Our Philosophy

Where Beauty Meets Innovation

Founded in 2015, KDJ Salon has grown from a small boutique to Colombo's premier beauty destination. Our founder, Kamala De Silva, envisioned a space where traditional beauty techniques meet cutting-edge technology.

We believe beauty is about enhancing your unique features, not masking them. Our team of internationally trained professionals stays ahead of trends while maintaining timeless elegance.

100% organic and cruelty-free products

Customized treatments for every skin and hair type

Hygiene and safety as top priorities

Our Commitment

We invest in continuous education for our staff, source sustainable products, and maintain an environment that makes every client feel valued and pampered.
